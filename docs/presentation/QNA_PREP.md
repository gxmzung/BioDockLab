# Q&A Preparation

## Is BioDockLab a medical device?

No. BioDockLab is a research software prototype. It is not a medical device or diagnostic tool.

## Does BioDockLab diagnose patients?

No. BioDockLab does not diagnose, treat, or recommend clinical decisions.

## What is the AI part?

The current AI layer is a rule-based analysis prototype for experiment risk, priority, and summary generation.

## What is the digital twin part?

The digital twin part is a simulation concept that estimates possible experimental response changes based on input parameters.

## What is the MVP?

The MVP is:

Experiment Data Registration → AI Risk Analysis → Digital Twin Prediction → Dashboard Visualization → Research Report Generation

## What needs expert review?

Bio terminology, experiment workflow realism, ethical boundaries, data assumptions, and future validation direction.
