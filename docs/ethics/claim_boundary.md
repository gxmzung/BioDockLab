# BioDockLab Claim Boundary

## Safe Claim
BioDockLab supports early-stage structure-based exploration, molecular visualization, docking-result management, and reproducible experiment logging.

## Unsafe Claim
BioDockLab must not claim that it can discover a drug, predict clinical efficacy, replace animal studies, replace human trials, or provide medical advice.

## Recommended Expression
- research support tool
- educational docking workflow platform
- early candidate prioritization support
- reproducible experiment management

## Ethics Position
Computational simulation can reduce unnecessary trial-and-error in early exploration, but wet-lab validation, toxicity evaluation, animal or alternative nonclinical studies, and clinical trials remain outside the scope of this project.
