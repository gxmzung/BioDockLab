# AI Claim Policy

## Purpose

This document defines how AI-related claims should be handled in BioDockLab.

## Allowed Claims

BioDockLab may claim:

- AI-assisted analysis concept
- Rule-based experiment analysis
- Risk classification prototype
- Research workflow support
- Digital twin-style simulation concept
- Report automation direction

## Claims to Avoid

BioDockLab should not claim:

- Medical diagnosis
- Clinical prediction
- Biological validation
- Treatment recommendation
- Real-world patient safety
- Certified AI model performance

## Current AI Status

The current AI layer is prototype-level and mostly rule-based.

It should be described as:

- Explainable
- Early-stage
- Research-oriented
- Not clinically validated

## Future Requirements

Before making stronger AI claims, BioDockLab would need:

- Real dataset validation
- Expert review
- Performance metrics
- Bias and safety review
- Legal and regulatory review
