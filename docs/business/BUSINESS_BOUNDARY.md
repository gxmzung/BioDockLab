# Business Boundary

## Purpose

This document defines the business boundary of BioDockLab.

## In Scope

BioDockLab may explore:

- Research workflow support
- Experiment data organization
- Report automation
- Lab software prototyping
- University startup support
- Research collaboration

## Out of Scope

BioDockLab does not currently provide:

- Medical diagnosis
- Clinical service
- Regulated medical software
- Hospital production system
- Certified bio prediction product

## Commercialization Caution

Any commercialization path must consider:

- Expert review
- Data protection
- Research validation
- Medical and bio ethics
- Legal and regulatory requirements

## Current Position

BioDockLab should currently be positioned as a research software prototype with startup potential, not as a validated medical or clinical product.
