# Cell-Free Protein Synthesis (CFPS)

## Overview

Cell-Free Protein Synthesis (CFPS) is a biological production method that synthesizes proteins without using living cells.

Instead of growing and maintaining entire cells, CFPS uses extracted cellular machinery such as enzymes, ribosomes, amino acids, and reaction systems to produce proteins directly.

CFPS is gaining attention because it can accelerate experimentation, reduce biological complexity, and simplify protein production workflows.

---

## Why CFPS Matters

CFPS offers several advantages:

* Faster experimental cycles
* Reduced cell-culture requirements
* Flexible reaction design
* Easier parameter control
* Rapid prototyping of biological experiments

Applications include:

* Synthetic biology
* Drug discovery
* Vaccine research
* Protein engineering
* Educational bioengineering systems
