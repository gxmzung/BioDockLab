# Surgery AI Research Direction

## Overview

Surgery AI is treated as a long-term healthcare data-flow extension for BioDockLab.

It is not the current implementation target.  
BioDockLab does not provide surgical decision-making, diagnosis, or clinical recommendation.

The purpose of this document is to organize how surgery-related data flow could be studied from a research software perspective.

---

## Possible Future Direction

Potential research software directions include:

- Medical data flow visualization
- Surgical workflow documentation
- Risk report generation
- Imaging data organization
- Clinical workflow simulation
- Role-based access and audit logs
