# Quantum Biocomputing Research Direction

## Overview

Quantum Biocomputing is a long-term research topic that explores how future quantum computing technologies may interact with biological research workflows.

This document exists to organize potential future research directions and does not represent a current implementation target of BioDockLab.

---

## Why It Matters

Biological systems generate large and complex datasets.

Future quantum computing technologies may contribute to:

- Molecular simulation
- Protein structure analysis
- Drug discovery
- Genome-scale computation
- Biological network analysis
- Complex optimization problems
