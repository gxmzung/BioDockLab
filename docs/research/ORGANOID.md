# Organoid Research

## Overview

Organoids are stem-cell-derived miniature organ models used to study disease mechanisms, drug responses, tissue development, and therapeutic candidates.

They provide researchers with an intermediate experimental model between traditional cell culture and human clinical testing.

BioDockLab treats organoid research as one of the long-term biological experiment domains that can benefit from structured experiment management, AI analysis, simulation, and reporting.

---

## Why Organoids Matter

Organoids are increasingly used in:

* Disease modeling
* Drug screening
* Personalized medicine
* Toxicity testing
* Regenerative medicine research
* Precision healthcare

Compared with traditional cell culture, organoids can better represent biological complexity.
