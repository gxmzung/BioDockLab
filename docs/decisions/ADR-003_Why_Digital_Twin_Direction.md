# ADR-003: Why Digital Twin Direction

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Biological experiments often require repeated trials, time, cost, and careful comparison between experimental conditions.

BioDockLab needs a long-term technical direction that connects experiment data, AI analysis, and simulation.

## Decision

BioDockLab adopts digital twin-style simulation as a long-term research direction.

## Reasons

- Connects data management with simulation
- Supports experiment comparison
- Provides future expansion potential
- Fits bio research workflows
- Helps explain experimental response changes
- Can support report generation

## Consequences

Positive:

- Strong long-term research direction
- Clear connection between AI, simulation, and reports
- Expandable architecture

Trade-offs:

- Requires careful validation
- Must not be presented as clinical prediction
- Needs real data and expert feedback for maturity
